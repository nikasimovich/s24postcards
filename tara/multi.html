Frank Ockenfels: The Beauty Behind the Introspection

Exploring the artist’s exhibit at Fotografiska, New York.

By Tara Lamorgese

“In my own photos too, I am comfortable with accepting blurriness and imperfection”

- Devin, amateur photographer

Photographer, Devin came to the exhibit with little knowledge surrounding Ockenfels. However, was quickly captivated by the artists’ storytelling techniques through mixing text, collages, and photographs. Devin enters his projects spontaneously and very rarely creates a plan. He welcomes obstacles and flaws similarly to Ockenfels.

“A theme in most of my work is being ok with the unexpected or if something goes wrong... And that’s why a lot of his stuff is standing out to me”
